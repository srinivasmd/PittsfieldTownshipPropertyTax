# 2026 Property Tax Appeal Data - Pittsfield Township

Washtenaw County, Michigan

Converted township assessment PDFs to searchable CSV format covering **all residential areas** in Pittsfield Township. Includes 3 years of data (2024-2026) for Sales Analysis, ECF (Economic Condition Factor) Analysis, and Land Analysis.

## What's Here

| Folder | Contents |
|--------|----------|
| `analysis/` | Township data converted to CSV (2024-2026), comprehensive analysis, and appeal guide template |

## Who Can Use This

Any Pittsfield Township homeowner appealing their 2026 property tax assessment. The CSVs contain data for **all residential areas** -- not just one subdivision. Look up your ECF area code on your assessment notice or at BSA Online (bsaonline.com/?uid=193).

## Key Deadlines

- **Board of Review:** March 10, 2026 at 5:00 PM
- **Michigan Tax Tribunal:** July 31, 2026

## Data Sources

All data comes from official Pittsfield Township documents available at pittsfield-mi.gov/2230/Property-Assessment-Data:

- Residential Sales Analysis (2024, 2025, 2026)
- Residential ECF Analysis (2024, 2025, 2026)
- Residential Land Analysis (2024, 2025, 2026)
